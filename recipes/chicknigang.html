Chicknigang

Description

When it comes to classic recipes, the motto “if it ain’t broke, don’t fix it” couldn’t be more applicable. Indeed, for these timeless dishes, staying faithful to original recipes really brings out the best possible flavors. One of the most appealing aspects of comfort food is its unchanging quality; just one bite brings out all the flavors of home that make it just so heartening and uplifting. Sinigang is one of these dishes that perfectly encapsulates Filipino comfort—and this chicknigang recipe brings it to a whole other level!

Ingredients

  • 3 cloves garlic chopped
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 1 thumb ginger crushed
  • 2 lbs. chicken cut into serving pieces
  • 1 quart water
  • 1 cup daikon radish labanos, wedged
  • 22 grams Knorr Sinigang sa Sampaloc Mix
  • 2 tomatoes halved
  • 2 long green pepper
  • 5 okras
  • 12 string beans cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons fish sauce
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil

Steps

  1. Heat cooking oil in a pot. Saute garlic, onion, and ginger.
  2. Add the chicken once the onion softens. Cook one side for 2 minutes. Turn it over and do the same on the opposite side.
  3. Pour water. Let boil.
  4. Add daikon radish and Knorr Sinigang sa Sampaloc Mix. Cover the pot and continue cooking using low to medium heat for 35 minutes.
  5. Add tomato, long green pepper, okra, and string beans. Cook for 8 to 12 minutes.
  6. Season with ground black pepper and fish sauce.
  7. Add spinach. Cook for 1 minute.
  8. Transfer to a serving bowl.
  9. Serve hot. Share and enjoy!
